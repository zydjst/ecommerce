WELCOME TO BEJEWELED!

and this is where you can get your SPARKLES ON!

Jewelry plays a multifaceted role in completing an outfit, going beyond just adding sparkle.
Here's why jewelry is important:

Elevates an Outfit:
Jewelry can take a basic outfit from casual to dressy. A statement necklace can elevate a simple t-shirt and jeans, while delicate earrings can add polish to a work outfit.
Expresses Personal Style:
Jewelry is a powerful tool for self-expression. You can choose pieces that reflect your personality, mood, or interests. Whether it's a bold, chunky necklace or a collection of dainty bracelets, your jewelry choices speak volumes.
Creates Balance and Proportion:
Jewelry can be used to create visual balance in an outfit. A long necklace can elongate a short torso, while a chunky belt can balance out a flowy dress.

Anklet Collections

  • Stud Earrings:
    These are small earrings with a post that goes through the earlobe and a backing to secure them in place.
  • Hoop Earrings:
    They can be simple or adorned with gemstones, pearls, or other embellishments.
  • Dangle Earrings:
    They are a popular choice for adding a touch of elegance or personality to an outfit.
  • Piercing Earrings:
    They can be simple and thin or come in more elaborate designs like chains, climbers that travel up the ear, or even crawlers that seem to snake around the cartilage.
  • Pendant Necklace:
    These necklaces feature a pendant, which is a small ornamental piece that hangs from the chain.
  • Lariat Necklace:
    A lariat necklace is a long necklace, often reaching the navel or below, that can be worn in a variety of ways.
  • Station Necklace:
    A station necklace features multiple beads, pearls, or other gemstones spaced evenly along the chain.
  • Choker Necklace:
    A choker is a short necklace that fits snugly around the base of the neck. They can be thin and delicate or thick and statement-making.
  • Stacking Ring:
    Stacking rings allow you to create a personalized look and add a touch of whimsy to your outfit.
  • Engagement Ring:
    Engagement rings are traditionally given by a person to their partner as a symbol of their commitment to getting married.
  • Promise Ring:
    Promise rings can be any style, but they are often simpler and less expensive than engagement rings.
  • Statement Ring:
    They can be large and chunky, or they can be more delicate but still have a unique and eye-catching design.
  • Cuff Bracelet:
    Similar to bangles, cuff bracelets are rigid but have an opening that allows you to slip them on your wrist.
  • Charm Bracelet:
    These sentimental bracelets feature a chain with links or clasps that can be used to attach charms.
  • Tennis Bracelet:
    These delicate bracelets feature a row of diamonds or other gemstones set in a flexible metal setting.
  • Chain Bracelet:
    Link bracelets can be chunky or delicate, and they can be plain or adorned with charms or other embellishments.
  • Chain Anklet:
    They can be worn alone for a minimalist look or layered with other anklets for a more bohemian vibe.
  • Charm Anklet:
    Similar to charm bracelets, charm anklets feature a chain with links or clasps that can be used to attach charms.
  • Seashell Anklet:
    A perfect reminder of summer days at the beach, seashell anklets are made from natural seashells strung together on a thread or cord.
  • Cuff Anklet:
    These stylish anklets have a curved band that fits snugly around the ankle. They can be plain or embellished with beads, charms, or other details.
